Project B: NetZone (Final Project of IAT339)

Netzone is the official reservation website for internet cafes. On Netzone, you can find out the detailed configuration of each type of computer in the Internet cafe, including CPU model, graphics card performance, memory size, etc., so that you can choose according to your own needs. The site also offers a wide range of product and service ordering options. In addition, you can choose your seat in advance through the online reservation function of the website.
